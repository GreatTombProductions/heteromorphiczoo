## What This Page Is NOT

- Not a press release. No "we're excited to announce."
- Not a biography. The Bestiary tells the band's story.
- Not a genre description. Nobody needs to be told what they're hearing.
- Not an explanation of the lineup change. The music IS the explanation. The dual-vocalist credit IS the statement. The Chronicle tells the history for those who want it.

The page is a single room. You enter, you hear the blessing, you join or you leave. Three things: the song, the invitation, the names of those who made it. Nothing else needs to be here.
